How it works

Your rating, earned on court.

RALLY tracks every match, calculates your Elo, and builds a performance identity that actually reflects how you play.

01

Log your match

Record the result after every game — singles or doubles, padel or tennis. Takes under 30 seconds.

02

Your Elo updates

Win against a stronger player and your rating climbs more. Beat someone weaker and it barely moves. Honest, dynamic, fair.

03

Know your level

See exactly where you stand, track your progress over time, and find opponents at your level.

Why Elo?

The same system used in chess, table tennis, and football rankings. It adjusts based on the strength of your opposition — so beating a top player matters more than beating a beginner. Your rating is a true reflection of your game, not just your win count.

About RALLY

Built by players, for players.

Padel is the fastest growing sport in the world. In Spain alone, there are over 5 million players and thousands of clubs — yet there is still no universal way to know your level. No shared rating. No objective benchmark. Just a game that keeps growing with no infrastructure to match it.

RALLY was Sascha's idea. He brought Iñaki on to lead the commercial side — and together they're building the performance identity layer for padel, starting in Madrid in 2026.

We're launching with a small group of founding players who'll help shape what RALLY becomes. If you care about your game, we'd love you to be part of it.
